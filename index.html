Inside the app

Built for the way people actually explore Coorg

Not a list of places — a working companion for the trip, from the first "where should we go today" to the drive back.

⛰️

Explore, filtered your way

Nearby, Free, Family or Adventure — two-tier filtering and search get you from "somewhere green" to Abbey Falls in a couple of taps.

🧭

A trip plan that makes sense

Trip Planner groups your saved spots by geography, so a day out covers one corner of Kodagu instead of zig-zagging across it.

☁️

Know before you go

Live weather for Coorg, pulled in real time, so you know if it's a waterfall day or a homestay-and-filter-coffee day.

❤️

Save it, plan it, find it again

Favourites carry across the app — tap once on Explore, and it's already sitting in your Trip Planner when you're ready to route.
